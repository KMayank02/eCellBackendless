ABOUT THE EVENT

The Entrepreneurship Cell (eCell) of NSUT is thrilled to announce the return of its national-level case study competition, "UNRAVEL" under its annual flagship event the Entrepreneurship Summit 2022.

As the name suggests, the competition is all about unraveling real-world business problems. Are you a budding entrepreneur? Do you want a chance to be in the limelight for your problem solving skills? Will you be able to sail through complicated corporate environments? If your answer is yes, then the wait's over!

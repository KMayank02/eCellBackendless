ABOUT THE EVENT

This E-Summit brings with it an event that will push you to think on your feet and put yourself through the arduous task of brainstorming and implementation. The Entrepreneurship Cell (eCell) of NSUT is thrilled to announce one of the most exciting events, Bellwether - The Perfect Manager, under its annual E-Summit 2022.

The event will be dealing with the ever-so-dynamic demand and supply of a product/service in the market and working on it to develop said product/ service.

Bellwether, as a simulation, greatly resembles real economic scenarios and makes use of one's managerial and analytical acumen to aid them to create a product, minimizing the cost, optimally utilizing the resources and time to develop something of great potential.
